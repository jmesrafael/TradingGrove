WEBSITE PURPOSE & MISSION
================================================================================
TradingGrove is a professional trading platform designed to help traders master risk
management and maintain consistent trading discipline. The platform combines free
calculators with a comprehensive trading journal, enabling traders to calculate
position sizes precisely before each trade and log results to discover patterns
and strategies that work.

Target Users:
- Cryptocurrency traders (Bitcoin, Altcoins, major digital assets)
- Forex traders (Currency pairs, commodities, indices)
- Beginner to professional traders seeking discipline and consistency

Core Philosophy:
Position sizing and risk management are the foundation of sustainable trading.
Most traders fail because they don't calculate risk before trading, leading to
over-leverage and blown accounts. TradingGrove eliminates guesswork and emotions
by providing exact calculations instantly.


MAIN FEATURES & FUNCTIONALITY
================================================================================

1. CRYPTO POSITION SIZE CALCULATOR
-----------------------------------
Purpose: Calculate exact position size, leverage requirements, and risk metrics
for cryptocurrency trades.

Key Inputs:
- Account Capital: Your total trading account balance in USD
- Risk Per Trade: Amount willing to lose per trade (% or fixed USD)
- Entry Price: Exact price at which you enter the trade
- Stop Loss: Distance from entry (price mode or % mode)
- Take Profit: Price target or % distance from entry
- Leverage: 1x to 500x (controls margin requirement)

Key Outputs:
- Risk (Max Loss): Exact dollar amount you'll lose if stopped out
- Target Profit: Exact profit if take profit is hit
- Capital Required (Margin): How much capital needed to hold position
- Risk/Reward Ratio: Ratio of potential loss to profit
- Leverage Display: Shows actual position control with chosen leverage

Real-Time Results:
- Calculations update instantly as you adjust inputs
- No signup required - completely free
- Results save to browser localStorage for quick access next session

Advantages Over Manual Calculation:
- Zero math errors
- Handles leverage automatically
- Shows exact margin requirement upfront
- Displays R:R ratio instantly
- Saves defaults for repeated calculations
- Speed: Get results in seconds instead of minutes


2. FOREX LOT SIZE CALCULATOR
----------------------------
Purpose: Calculate exact lot size, pip value, and risk metrics for forex pairs
across all major, minor, and exotic pairs.

Key Inputs:
- Currency Pair: Select from 30+ major and minor forex pairs
- Account Currency: Base currency of your trading account
- Account Balance: Total account balance in account currency
- Risk Per Trade: % of account or fixed dollar amount
- Entry Price: Exact entry level on the chart
- Stop Loss: Pips distance or exact price level
- Take Profit: Pips distance or exact price level

Key Outputs:
- Money at Risk: Dollar amount lost if stopped out
- Lot Size: Micro (1K), Mini (10K), or Standard (100K) lot type
- Units: Exact number of units for the calculated lot size
- Pip Value: Dollar value per pip for chosen pair and lot size
- Target Profit: Exact profit if take profit level is hit
- Pip Value (Total): Total pip value for your full position
- Risk/Reward Ratio: Statistical edge calculator

Advanced Features:
- Automatic pip detection for all pairs (EUR/USD = 0.0001 pip, USD/JPY = 0.01)
- Handles cross pairs and exotic currencies
- Shows SL distance in pips for reference
- Displays contract specifications per pair
- P&L section reveals both risk and reward sides

Advantages Over Manual Calculation:
- Eliminates pip value errors (most common mistake)
- Automatic conversion for all account currencies
- Handles pair-specific pip decimals
- Shows cleanest lot size option
- Instantly reveals if position is viable for account size
