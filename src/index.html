Most traders have more data
than they realise they do.

Your trade history already holds patterns worth knowing — the times you trade well, the emotions that lead to mistakes, the setups that actually pay. TradingGrove helps you read that history clearly.

Your mood has more impact on results than you might expect

It's not that emotional awareness is a secret — most traders know it matters. What's harder is seeing the actual numbers. When you can compare your calm sessions against your frustrated ones in a simple chart, it becomes much easier to act on what you find.

Mood vs Performance chart reveals this over time

Some hours of the day may suit your trading better than others

This varies for every trader. Some find they're sharper in the morning. Others notice they make more impulsive decisions late in the afternoon. Looking at an hour-by-hour breakdown of your own results is one of the more practical things you can do early on.

Hour Heatmap shows your personal performance by time of day

The setup you use most often isn't always the one that fits you best

It's easy to keep returning to familiar setups without checking whether they're actually performing. Comparing your strategies side by side — wins, losses, and total result — gives you a clearer picture of where your time is best spent.

Strategy PNL breakdown puts everything side by side

A few large losses can quietly undo a lot of steady progress

It's one thing to know your win rate. It's another to see your equity curve and notice the shape of it. Are the drawdowns clustered? Are you recovering steadily or in bursts? These questions are easier to answer when you can actually see the picture.

Equity Curve makes your progress and setbacks visible

Common questions.

Is TradingGrove really free?
Yes. The Free plan is free forever — 1 journal, basic analytics, unlimited trades, no credit card required. The Pro plan is $15/month (or ~$10/month billed annually) and unlocks unlimited journals, the full analytics suite, screenshot gallery, export/import, themes, PIN lock, and more. Cancel anytime.
Where is my data stored?
Your trades are stored securely in our cloud database powered by Supabase with row-level security — meaning only you can ever access your journals and trade data. We never sell your data or use it for any purpose beyond running your journal.
Can I use TradingGrove on multiple devices?
Yes — sign in on any device (phone, tablet, desktop) and your trades, journals, notes, and settings are there instantly. No manual syncing, no exports needed. This works on both Free and Pro plans.
Can I track multiple trading accounts separately?
Pro users can create unlimited journals — one for Forex, one for Crypto, one for a funded account, and so on. Each journal has its own separate trade history, analytics, PIN, default tags, and capital. Free users get 1 journal.
What analytics does the Free plan include?
Free users get the core stats (Total PNL, Win Rate, Wins, Losses, Avg R, Best/Worst trade), win and loss streak counters, and a full equity curve chart. Pro unlocks everything else: Strategy PNL, Mood charts, Hour analysis, Day-of-week breakdown, Long vs Short, Timeframe breakdown, and Most Traded Pairs.
Can I export my trade data?
Yes — Pro users can export a full journal backup as JSON (trades + all settings + notes) or as a CSV for use in spreadsheets. You can also import from TradingGrove JSON or from CSV exports from Binance, Bybit, or MT4. Your data is always yours.
What happens to my data if I cancel Pro?
Your trades are never deleted. If you cancel, you revert to the Free plan limits — 1 journal visible, basic analytics, 90-day calendar. Your data stays safe and you can re-upgrade at any time to access everything again.
