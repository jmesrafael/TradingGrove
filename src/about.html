01

Why TradingGrove exists

Most traders don't fail because they pick the wrong setup. They fail because they size positions emotionally, skip the journal when results hurt, and never see their own patterns until the account is gone.

Position sizing and journaling are the two unsexy, unavoidable habits behind every consistent trader. We built TradingGrove to make both of them fast enough to actually do — calculators that take three inputs, a journal that opens to the right row, analytics that surface the truth without needing a data science degree.

No signals. No "guru" picks. No advice. Just the tools we wish we'd had on day one.
