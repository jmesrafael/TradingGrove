WEBSITE PURPOSE & MISSION
================================================================================
TradingGrove is a professional trading platform designed to help traders master risk
management and maintain consistent trading discipline. The platform combines free
position-sizing calculators with a full cloud-synced trading journal, enabling
traders to calculate position sizes precisely before each trade, log every result,
and use built-in analytics to discover which patterns and strategies actually work.

Target Users:
- Cryptocurrency traders (Bitcoin, Altcoins, major digital assets)
- Forex traders (Currency pairs, commodities, indices)
- Beginner to professional traders seeking discipline and consistency

Core Philosophy:
Position sizing and risk management are the foundation of sustainable trading.
Most traders fail because they don't calculate risk before trading, leading to
over-leverage and blown accounts. TradingGrove eliminates guesswork and emotions
by providing exact calculations instantly, then closes the loop with a journal
that proves (with data) whether the trader is actually following their plan.


MAIN FEATURES & FUNCTIONALITY
================================================================================

1. POSITION CALCULATOR (Crypto / Forex toggle)
------------------------------------------------
Purpose: Calculate exact position size, leverage requirements, and risk metrics
for crypto and forex trades from one combined tool. A dedicated standalone
Crypto Calculator page is also available.

Crypto Mode - Key Inputs:
- Account Capital: Total trading account balance in USD
- Risk Per Trade: % of account or fixed USD amount (toggle)
- Entry Price: Exact price at which you enter the trade
- Stop Loss: Price or % distance from entry (toggle)
- Take Profit: Price or % distance from entry (toggle)
- Leverage: 1x to 500x

Crypto Mode - Key Outputs:
- Risk (Max Loss): Exact dollar amount lost if stopped out
- Target Profit: Exact profit if take profit is hit
- Capital Required (Margin): Capital needed to hold the position
- Risk/Reward Meter: Poor / Average / Good / Great (3R+) rating, not just a raw ratio

Forex Mode - Key Inputs:
- Currency Pair: Majors, minors/crosses, metals (XAU/XAG), and indices (US30, NAS100)
- Account Currency: 8 supported account currencies
- Account Balance, Risk % or fixed $ (toggle)
- Entry Price, Stop Loss and Take Profit in pips or exact price (toggle)

Forex Mode - Key Outputs:
- Money at Risk, Lot Size, Units, Pip Value, Target Profit

Shared Features:
- Real-time recalculation as inputs change
- No signup required, free and unlimited
- "Set Default" saves risk settings for next session
- Zero math errors, instant margin/pip-value visibility


2. TRADING JOURNAL (fully built, cloud-synced)
------------------------------------------------
Purpose: Log every trade, plan sessions before trading, and let built-in
analytics reveal what's actually working. This is a complete SPA-style app
(not a "coming soon" feature) reached via /journal, with a sidebar covering:

- Logs: Trade entry/edit with date & time, pair, position (long/short),
  strategy tags, timeframe tags, PnL, R-factor, confidence rating (1-10),
  mood tags, free-text notes, pinning, and optional screenshot attachments
  (Pro tier, stored in Cloudflare R2 via presigned uploads)
- Pre-Session: Pre-trade checklist, mood/bias capture, and trade "intents"
  that can later be linked to the trades actually taken
- Calendar: Day-by-day view of trading activity and results
- Notes: Freeform custom notes tied to the journal
- Analytics: Win rate, profit factor, win/loss streaks, average
  winner/average loser, rolling win rate (last 20 trades), and breakdowns
  by timeframe, hour, mood, and confidence, plus auto-generated insight
  text (e.g., outlier-loss detection, confidence-calibration checks)
- Settings: Per-journal configuration, including optional PIN protection
  and an equity display toggle

Multiple Journals:
- 1 journal on the Free plan, unlimited journals on Pro
- Each journal can be independently PIN-protected

How It Helps:
- Reveals which setups actually work, backed by real analytics (not guesses)
- Identifies emotional trade patterns via mood/confidence tracking
- Shows real profitability across every logged trade
- Highlights best performing pairs, timeframes, and strategies
- Enables course corrections based on data, not gut feel
- Builds accountability and discipline through pre-session planning


3. EDUCATIONAL & CONTENT PAGES
------------------------------------------------
- About page

Risk Management Fundamentals (still core teaching points):

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

Stack:
- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS front end (no React/Vue framework) - hand-rolled DOM
  manipulation, per-page JS modules in src/js/modules/
- Build step (build.js) flattens src/ into public/ for deployment
- Hosted on Vercel

Backend & Data:
- Supabase (Postgres + Row Level Security on every user table)
- Core tables: profiles, journals, journal_settings, trades, trade_images,
  custom_notes, pre_sessions, presession_checklist_sets/items/state,
  trade_intents, referrals
- Trade screenshots stored in Cloudflare R2 via a Supabase Edge Function
  issuing presigned upload URLs
- Additional edge functions: stripe-webhook, paypal-webhook, create-checkout,
  create-paypal-subscription, billing-portal, apply-referral,
  grant-referral-reward, delete-account
- Cloud sync is now standard - data lives in Supabase, not just browser
  localStorage (calculators still use localStorage for saved defaults)

Authentication:
- Supabase Auth: email/password plus Google OAuth
- Password reset and email confirmation flows

Supported Markets:
CRYPTO:
- Any crypto/USD pair
- Leverage support: 1x to 500x

FOREX:
- Majors, minors/crosses, metals (XAU/XAG), indices (US30, NAS100)
- Account currencies: 8 supported (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CHF, AUD, CAD, NZD)
- Automatic pip detection per pair
