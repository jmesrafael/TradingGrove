Getting started
Is TradingGrove really free?
Yes. The Free plan gives you 1 journal, core analytics and unlimited trades, with no credit card and no expiry. Pro unlocks unlimited journals, the full analytics suite, screenshot gallery, export/import, themes, PIN lock, and more. Current prices and billing options live on the pricing page. Cancel anytime.
What is a trading journal, and why keep one?
A trading journal is a structured record of every trade you take: the setup, the size, the result, and the state of mind behind it. Its value shows up in review. After a few weeks of honest logging, patterns appear that memory alone will never surface, like which hours lose you money, which strategy carries your account, or how tilt shows up in your results. Most profitable traders journal for exactly this reason: profitability comes from expectancy and risk management, and you cannot manage what you never measure.
Which markets can I journal?
Any market where you take discrete trades: crypto spot and futures, forex, indices, stocks or commodities. You define your own pairs, strategies and tags, so the journal adapts to how you trade. The built-in position size calculator currently supports crypto, with a forex calculator planned.
How long does setup take?
Under a minute. Sign up with an email address, name your journal, and log your first trade. You can add default pairs, strategies and mood tags later as your logging habit settles in.
Plans & billing
What is the difference between Monthly and Annual?
Both include exactly the same Pro features. Annual billing is charged once per year at a meaningful discount compared to paying month by month. The current prices for both options are on the pricing page.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from Manage Billing whenever you like. You keep full Pro access until the end of the billing period, then revert to the Free plan. Nothing is deleted.

Your trades are never deleted. If you cancel, you revert to the Free plan limits: 1 journal visible, core analytics, 90-day calendar. Your full history stays intact and everything unlocks again if you re-upgrade.

Your data & devices
Can I use TradingGrove on multiple devices?
Yes. Sign in on any device (phone, tablet, desktop) and your trades, journals, notes and settings are there instantly. No manual syncing, no exports needed. This works on both Free and Pro plans.
Where is my data stored?
Your trades are stored in a cloud database powered by Supabase with row-level security, meaning only your account can read your journals and trade data. We never sell your data or use it for anything beyond running your journal. See the privacy policy for the full picture.
Can I export my trade data?
Yes. Pro users can export a full journal backup as JSON (trades, settings and notes) or as CSV for spreadsheets. You can also import from a TradingGrove JSON backup or from CSV exports from Binance, Bybit or MT4. Your data is always yours to take with you.
Can I track multiple trading accounts separately?
Pro users can create unlimited journals: one for spot, one for futures, one for a funded account, and so on. Each journal keeps its own trade history, analytics, PIN, default tags and capital. Free users get 1 journal.
Journaling & analytics
What analytics does the Free plan include?
Free users get the core stats (Total PNL, Win Rate, Wins, Losses, Avg R, Best/Worst trade), win and loss streak counters, and a full equity curve chart. Pro unlocks everything else: Strategy PNL, Mood charts, Hour analysis, Day-of-week breakdown, Long vs Short, Timeframe breakdown, and Most Traded Pairs.
Do I need a high win rate to be profitable?
No, and this is one of the most useful things a journal teaches. A trader who wins 41% of the time but averages 2.7R on winners against 1R on losers is comfortably profitable, while a 60% win rate with oversized losses can bleed an account. What matters is expectancy: how much you make per trade on average once wins and losses are weighed together. Your journal computes this from real data instead of leaving it to feel.
What should I record for each trade?
TradingGrove captures the details that turn into insight later: pair, direction, strategy, timeframe, PNL, R-multiple, confidence and mood, plus optional notes and chart screenshots. Each field feeds a specific chart, so the mood you tag today becomes next month's Mood vs Performance breakdown.
How is this better than a spreadsheet?
A spreadsheet stores numbers; you have to build and maintain every formula and chart yourself, and most traders stop updating it within weeks. TradingGrove computes the analytics automatically on every logged trade, syncs across devices, holds your screenshots next to the trade they belong to, and includes planning and review tools a spreadsheet was never designed for.
