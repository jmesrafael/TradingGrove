Spreadsheets store trades.
A journal improves them.

A spreadsheet can hold your numbers, but it will never tell you which hours drain your account, which setup deserves more size, or how your mood shows up in your results. A proper trade journal does. Here is what your own history can answer.

Which emotions cost you money

Every trader knows tilt exists. Very few know what it costs them per month. Tag each trade with how you felt and the Mood vs Performance chart puts a number on it. Once revenge trades have a dollar figure attached, they get much harder to justify.

Mood vs Performance chart

Which hours actually suit you

Morning trader or night owl, your results are not evenly spread across the day. The hour heatmap breaks your average PNL down by time of day, so you can stop giving back your best session's profit during your worst one.

Hour Performance heatmap

Which setup earns its place

The setup you trade most is not always the one that pays. Comparing strategies side by side, with wins, losses and total result, shows where your edge really is. Some traders discover their favourite setup has been subsidised by a boring one for months.

Strategy PNL breakdown

Whether your risk management is holding up

Win rate alone says very little. Your equity curve shows the shape of your trading: how deep the drawdowns go, how you recover, and whether a few oversized losses keep undoing weeks of discipline. That shape is where risk management problems show up first.

Equity Curve and drawdown view

Common questions.

Is TradingGrove really free?
Yes. The Free plan gives you 1 journal, core analytics and unlimited trades, with no credit card and no expiry. Pro unlocks unlimited journals, the full analytics suite, screenshot gallery, export/import, themes and PIN lock. Current plans and billing options are on the pricing page. Cancel anytime.
Can I use TradingGrove on multiple devices?
Yes. Sign in on any device (phone, tablet, desktop) and your trades, journals, notes, and settings are there instantly. No manual syncing, no exports needed. This works on both Free and Pro plans.
What analytics does the Free plan include?
Free users get the core stats (Total PNL, Win Rate, Wins, Losses, Avg R, Best/Worst trade), win and loss streak counters, and a full equity curve chart. Pro unlocks everything else: Strategy PNL, Mood charts, Hour analysis, Day-of-week breakdown, Long vs Short, Timeframe breakdown, and Most Traded Pairs.
What happens to my data if I cancel Pro?
Your trades are never deleted. If you cancel, you revert to the Free plan limits: 1 journal visible, core analytics, 90-day calendar. Your data stays intact and you can re-upgrade at any time to access everything again.
