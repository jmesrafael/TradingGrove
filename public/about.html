TradingGrove exists because we were tired of opening a spreadsheet at 3am, squinting at columns, and still not knowing why we lost. This page is the story, the philosophy, and where the product is headed.

01 · Why it exists

Most traders never see their own patterns

Traders rarely fail because they picked the wrong setup. They fail because they size positions emotionally, skip the journal when results hurt, and never see their own patterns until the account is gone. The information was always there, sitting in their own trade history; nobody made it readable.

Position sizing and journaling are the two unglamorous, unavoidable habits behind every consistent trader. We built TradingGrove to make both of them fast enough to actually do: a risk calculator that takes three inputs, a trade log that takes thirty seconds, and trading analytics that surface the truth without a data science degree.

The product is opinionated about one thing above all: profitability comes from expectancy and risk management, not from a high win rate. A trader who loses 6 trades out of 10 and still ends the month green is not an anomaly; that is what disciplined trading usually looks like. Our analytics, our demo data and our copy are all built to tell that story honestly.

02 · Philosophy

A tool, not a guru

No signals. No picks. No "one weird pattern" content. No promises about returns. TradingGrove will never tell you what to trade, because the entire point of journaling is learning to read your own evidence and make your own decisions.

We also keep the product deliberately small. Every feature has to serve the loop of plan, log, review; anything that does not gets cut. A journal you actually keep beats a platform with a hundred half-features.

03 · What we believe

Three commitments

Your data is yours

We never sell your journal, share it, or train AI on it. Export everything as JSON or CSV whenever you want. Delete your account and it is gone permanently.

No financial advice, ever

TradingGrove is a tool, not an adviser. Every decision on your account is yours alone. That is not a disclaimer, it is the design principle.

A free journal that stays free

One journal, the calculators and core analytics cost nothing for as long as TradingGrove exists. Pro exists for traders who outgrow it, not to hold the basics hostage.
